# Apple Health Imports

## What to drop here
Your Apple Health **export.zip** file.

## How to export from Apple Health
1. Open the **Health** app on your iPhone
2. Tap your profile picture (top right)
3. Scroll down and tap **"Export All Health Data"**
4. Tap **"Export"** and wait — this can take a minute
5. Share or AirDrop the resulting **export.zip** to your Mac
6. Drop the zip file into this folder

## What happens next
A future import script will read this file and pull in metrics like:
- Resting heart rate
- HRV (Heart Rate Variability)
- Body weight
- Step count
- Sleep data

## Important
Do not rename the file — leave it as `export.zip`.
You can drop a fresh export here any time to update your data.
